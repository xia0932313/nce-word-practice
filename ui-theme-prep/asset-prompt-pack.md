# Asset Prompt Pack

这些提示词用于后续生成原创背景图、角色图、怪物图、武器/道具图。所有提示词都明确要求不要包含受版权保护的角色、标志、独特武器或可识别图案。

## 通用约束

追加到每条提示词末尾：

```text
Original design only. Do not include or imitate any copyrighted character, logo, emblem, named weapon, named gadget, or recognizable franchise symbol. No text, no watermark, no signature.
```

透明素材建议先生成纯色背景版本：

```text
Create the subject on a perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background for later background removal. The background must be one uniform color with no shadow, gradient, texture, floor plane, or lighting variation. Keep the subject fully separated from the background with crisp edges and generous padding. Do not use #00ff00 anywhere in the subject.
```
